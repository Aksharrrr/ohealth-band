Sensor Intelligence
Six Layers of Human Data
Every critical human metric — from air quality to cardiac rhythm — captured in a single wrist-worn device.
BME688 · Gas + Pressure + Humidity

Air Quality Index

Real-time VOC, CO₂-equiv, temp & humidity monitoring. First AQI sensor in wristband form. Flags dangerous exposure before performance impact.

AS7341 · Spectral Light Sensor

UV Exposure Intel

Monitors real-time UV-A/B with personalized skin-damage risk. Calculates score based on skin type and cumulative exposure.

Synchronized ECG & PPG AFE

Cardiovascular Precision

Clinical-grade analog front-end. ECG+PPG synchronization enables pulse transit time for non-invasive BP estimation. On-device arrhythmia detection via ML.

Proprietary Bioimpedance Algorithm

Hydration Score Engine

Objective real-time hydration scores — not estimates. Data-driven fluid intake guidance prevents dehydration in athletes and post-surgical patients.

Thermistor + Proprietary Algorithm

Fever & Infection Flag

Continuous body temperature with fever flagging and early infection detection. Enables proactive medical intervention before symptoms escalate.

BMP585 · High-Precision Barometer

Altitude & Pressure

Adds environmental context to all activity data. Altitude for mountain sports, floor detection for indoor navigation, and physiological contextualization.
